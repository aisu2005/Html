Kerala, the "God's Own Country" looks even more beatiful in monsoon season.The heavy rainfall, high humidity, lush green landscapes, and are considered ideal for experiencing nature and traditional Ayurvedic treatments.

Monsoon Timing Characteristics

  • Southwest Monsoon(Edavappathi)
  1. June to August/September
  2. significant rainfall,humid temperatures
  • Northeast Monsoon(Thulavarsham)
  1. October to November
  2. moderate rainfall

Key Features of Kerala's Monsoon

  • Greenery
  • Ayurveda treatments
  • Weather
